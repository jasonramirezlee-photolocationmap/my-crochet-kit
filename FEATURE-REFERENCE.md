## üî• CRITICAL PAIN POINTS (Must-Have Features)

### 1. Pattern Organization is TERRIBLE in Other Apps
**The Problem:**
- Apps resort patterns in random ways
- Can't search by name
- Patterns disappear when switching apps
- No way to tag or categorize

**Our Solution:**
- Smart search by name, project type, yarn weight
- Custom folders and tags
- Multiple sorting options (date, name, status)
- Never lose anything!

---

### 2. Pattern Reading Feels Like "A Foreign Language"
**The Problem:**
- US vs UK terms confusion
- Complex abbreviations
- Hard to keep your place
- Patterns close and you lose your spot

**Our Solution:**
- Built-in US/UK term converter
- Persistent highlighting that NEVER disappears
- Pattern annotation (circle, highlight, underline)
- Auto-save your place in every pattern

---

### 3. Row Counters That Actually WORK
**The Problem:**
- Counters reset randomly (data loss!)
- Can't keep pattern visible while counting
- No custom alerts
- App closes and you lose count

**Our Solution:**
- Multiple counters per project
- Custom alerts: "Row 15: Change to blue yarn!"
- Counter visible WITH pattern on same screen
- Auto-save every click
- Works offline (critical!)

---

### 4. HAND PAIN is HUGE
**The Problem:**
- Marathon crochet sessions cause injury
- Carpal tunnel, cramping, neck/back pain
- No reminders to take breaks

**Our Solution:**
- Smart break reminders every 20-30 minutes
- Ergonomic tips popup
- Track crochet time automatically
- "You've been crocheting for 2 hours - time for a break!"

---

### 5. Yarn Stash Tracking is a NIGHTMARE
**The Problem:**
- Hard to log and organize
- Doesn't link to projects automatically
- Manual updates required
- Barcode scanning doesn't work

**Our Solution:**
- Easy yarn entry (photo + details)
- Automatic project linking
- Yarn usage calculator per project
- Working barcode scanner
- "Do I have enough for this project?" calculator

---

### 6. Offline Mode is NON-NEGOTIABLE
**The Problem:**
- Crocheters travel, go to cafes, have spotty service
- Patterns disappear without internet
- Data loss is common

**Our Solution:**
- Everything works offline (PWA magic!)
- Auto-sync when online
- Cloud backup (never lose data)
- "Last synced: 2 minutes ago"

---

### 7. Pricing for Sellers (HUGE Opportunity!)
**The Problem:**
- Crocheters who sell undercharge
- Don't know how to calculate materials + time + profit
- No time tracking

**Our Solution:**
- Automatic time tracking per project
- Materials cost calculator
- Labor cost (hourly rate √ó hours)
- Overhead calculator
- Profit margin suggestions
- "You should charge $45 for this blanket"
